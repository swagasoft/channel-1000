The structure

The customer registers 2 persons below his or her down-line

Where the 2 down-lines were registered by a marketer;

For N1,000 option, the company pays N125 each for the two down lines (i.e. N250) to the marketer(s) that registered the down lines and takes N125 each for the two down lines (i.e. N250) as service charge and pays the customer a bonus of N1,500. This gives a total of the N2,000.

For N2,000 option, the company pays N250 each for the two down lines (i.e. N500) to the marketer(s) that registered the down lines and takes N250 each for the two down lines (i.e. N500) as service charge and pays the customer a bonus of N3,000. This gives a total of the N4,000.

For N5,000 option, the company pays N500 each for the two down lines (i.e. N1000) to the marketer(s) that registered the down lines and takes N750 each for the two down lines (i.e. N1,500) as service charge and pays the customer a bonus of N7,500. This gives a total of the N10,000.

For N10,000 option, the company pays N1,000 each for the two down lines (i.e. N2,000) to the marketer(s) that registered the down lines and takes N1,500 each for the two down lines (i.e. N3,000) as service charge and pays the customer a bonus of N15,000. This gives a total of the N20,000.
