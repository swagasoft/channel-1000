Can I register as a marketer as well as a customer?

Yes, although with different user names and email.

Apart from my account, can I also have accounts for my children?

Yes. Our platform has products that can benefit your children especially those writing WAEC and JAMB.

If I forget my password, what will I do?

Where you lose your password, click on forget password and a One Time Password (OTP) will be sent to the email address you used during your registration.

What happens if I wish to update my account details?

Log in to your account and make any needed update you desired example your account number, bank name and account name.

At what point will I be paid?

The moment you have up to N1000 in your account.

Are there other ways of earning cash on the platform?

It is either you are earning as a marketer or you are earning as a customer.
