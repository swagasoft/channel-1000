ABOUT US

Channel 1000 was established in other to combat the menace of unemployment and poverty in the country. This conception was instigated owing to a close view of Nigerian education system vis-a-vis the rate at which universities push these students into Labour market.

An analytical view of our education system revealed that Nigeria produces about 300,000 graduates year. Further analysis shows that at absorption rate of 20 graduates in a company, it would take about 15,000 companies yearly to have these graduates off the street.
On the contrary, these graduates increase the size of labour market on yearly basis as the system has no jobs for them.

It was on this note that Channel 1000 while having a road map to channel 1000 small businesses into the system on yearly basis to help reduce this stress on the system, also wishes to share profit made from other businesses they do with their subscribes.

We also have an affiliate marketing platform where these graduates who wish to work but are unable to get an immediate employment can register.
